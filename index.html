Research

I am currently working on large-scale optimization for statistical machine learning in the context of sparse graph inference.

A bit more formally, given an empirical covariance matrix \(S \in \mathbb{R}^{p \times p}\) of a dataset of \(n\) random vectors \( x_i \sim \mathcal{N}^p(\mu, \Sigma) \), I am interested in estimating \( \Theta = \Sigma^{-1}\) (called precision matrix) with a sparsity prior. This is important because \( \Theta \) can be seen as the adjacency matrix of a graph modelling the correlation between considered entities over time.

I have a particular interest in applications to neuroscience problems.
