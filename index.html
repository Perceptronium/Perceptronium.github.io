Research

I am currently working on large-scale optimization for statistical machine learning in the context of sparse graph learning.

A bit more formally, given an empirical covariance matrix \(S \in \mathbb{R}^{p \times p}\) of a dataset of \(n\) random vectors \( x_i \sim \mathcal{N}^p(\mu, \Sigma) \), I am interested in estimating \( \Theta = \Sigma^{-1}\) (called precision matrix) with a sparsity prior. I am currently trying to bridge the gap between convex optimization-based approaches and deep learning-based approaches to do so.

I have a particular interest in applications to neuroscience problems.
